About Me

I grew up in the Midwest in a small town in Illinois. After high school, I went into Nursing, and was in that career for 23 years. After surviving an F5 tornado, I moved to Arizona and lived there for 15 years. I started working at Walmart January of 2013 and still work for them today. I transferred to the U.P. to help my sister take care of our mother. We live out in the country and close to Lake Michigan. I enjoy developing computer applications and learning new technology to keep up with the fast changing field. I love crafts of all kinds. I raise African Grey Parrots.
